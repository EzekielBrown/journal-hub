Product Planning

Week 3

Product planning is creating organised objectives for your team to follow to help you focus on achieving your goal. It helps you to identify and measure the progress you have made through the project. Product planning has many layers to it from start to finish. Prioritisation is important to product planning as it shows what needs to be urgently created/fixed and what does not need to be created/fixed right away. Key things to remember when deciding on the more important items were how many users are affected, how each user will be affected, how confident we are to achieving the goal, and how much effort will be going into this issue.
