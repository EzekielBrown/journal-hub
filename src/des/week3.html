Product Planning

Product planning gives you a clear road map of what needs to be achieve by you and your team. It is necessary to part building a product to achieve efficiency and to lower cost of research and development time. With planning, you will develop a list of tasks and side tasks that will need to be completed. You will organized them by how important they are. Clear instructions of what the task entails will need to be provided as well so that all members knows what going on. Github and Devops have useful tools that will allow you to create jobs and task and will allow you to assign them to people in your team, can be easily moved around and can set tags and priority levels on each task. Roadmap Roadmapping is a very useful tool to show the direction of a company or a product is heading in. Roadmaps gives a step by step guide for what will be happening for the future. It provides transparency with the client and stakeholders.
